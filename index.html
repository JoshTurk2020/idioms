Idiom Story

Once upon a time, there was a bull in a china shop who wanted to learn some new English idioms. One day, they decided to jump the gun and started reading a book about them.

As they read, they realized that some idioms were as clear as day, while others were as clear as mud. They were determined to get to the bottom of it.

After some time, the bull in a china shop started to get the ball rolling and began to wrap their head around the meanings of the idioms.
